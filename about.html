Our Story

helloshop was born from a simple passion for exceptional coffee. What started as a small cart in a local farmers' market has grown into the warm, inviting space you see today.

Our journey began in 2015 when our founder, Emma, returned from traveling through coffee-growing regions around the world. Inspired by the rich traditions and community-centered approach to coffee she experienced, Emma was determined to create a space that honored the craft of coffee making while fostering connections among people.

At helloshop, we believe that great coffee is about more than just beans and brewing methods. It's about creating moments of joy, fostering community, and providing a space where everyone feels welcome. Every cup we serve is crafted with care, using ethically sourced beans from small-scale farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our team of passionate baristas is dedicated to the art and science of coffee. We're constantly learning, experimenting, and refining our craft to bring you the perfect cup every time. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur or just looking for a cozy place to work or meet friends, we're here to make your experience special.

Thank you for being part of our story. We look forward to serving you and growing together as a community.
